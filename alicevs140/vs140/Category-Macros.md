# Category Macros
These macros define category maps.  
  
|||  
|-|-|  
|[BEGIN_CATEGORY_MAP](../vs140/BEGIN_CATEGORY_MAP.md)|Marks the beginning of the category map.|  
|[END_CATEGORY_MAP](../vs140/END_CATEGORY_MAP.md)|Marks the end of the category map.|  
|[IMPLEMENTED_CATEGORY](../vs140/IMPLEMENTED_CATEGORY.md)|Indicates categories that are implemented by the COM object.|  
|[REQUIRED_CATEGORY](../vs140/REQUIRED_CATEGORY.md)|Indicates categories that are required of the container by the COM object.|
