# Displaying or Hiding the Colors Window (Image Editor for Icons)
### To display Colors window  
  
1.  Right-click in an **Image Editor** pane and choose **Show Colors Window** from the shortcut menu.  
  
     \- or -  
  
2.  Click **Show Colors Window** on the [Image menu](../vs140/Image-Menu--Image-Editor-for-Icons-.md).  
  
### To hide the Colors window  
  
1.  Unpin the window. This will allow the window to auto hide when it's not in use. For more information, see [Arranging Windows](../vs140/How-to--Arrange-and-Dock-Windows.md).  
  
 \- or -  
  
-   Click the **Close** button.  
  
 Requirements  
  
 None
